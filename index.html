No Pain! No Gain! Self-discipline makes me happy and strong.

Hello! My name is Hao.

I love bodybuilding and hiking. This website is my bodybuilding plan. I often practice four times every week(not during the busy final weeks).

I like to work out two more body parts every training.
  • 1st: Chest & Delts & Triceps;
  • 2nd: Back & Biceps;
  • 3rd: Legs & Abs;
  • 4th: Return to 1st and loop

Chest:

  • Incline Bench Press
  • Barbell Bench Press
  • Cable crossover
  • Bar Dips

Back:

  • Pull up
  • Wide-grip Pull down
  • Barbell Bent-over Row
  • Deadlift

Delts:

  • Shoulder Press
  • Lateral Dumbbell Raise
  • Face Pull

Arms:

  • Standing Barbell Curl
  • AlternateDumbbell Curl
  • Lying Triceps Extension
  • Dumbbell Kickback
  • Cable Press Down

Legs:

  • Deep Squat/Hack Squat
  • Leg Extension/Lunge
  • Lying Leg Curl
  • Toe Raise

Abs:

  • Rope Crunch
  • Reverse Crunch
  • Parallel bar leg raise (knee ups)
